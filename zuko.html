TELLING THE STORY OF ZUKO

This project focuses on telling the story of an existing character using an image, mise en scene, and animation. The project is divided into 3 stages:

  • Stage 1: Still Scene
  • Stage 2: 30 second animated scene
  • Stage 3: Interactive montage

Technology used: Maya and Unreal

This was one of my favorite projects because the end product went beyond expectation. I came into the course not knowing anything about creating 3D-objects or animation. I spent long nights learning animation programs, but the whole process was worth it. I learned the importance of focusing on every detail, and how each detail of a scene/screen can affect the audience.

STAGE 1

Objective: Create a scene in Maya filled with at least 5 hero objects that are modeled and textured in a way that portrays the character.

I chose to portray Zuko from Avatar: The Last Airbender series because he has the ultimate character arc. He starts out as the main villain of the show, but towards the end of the series he becomes a major ally for the protagonist. His story is defined by his journey of finding his purpose and self-worth.

Mood Board

Objects to Portray character:

  1. Candle:
    Zuko comes from the Fire Nation. It represents who he is, and where he came from.
  2. White Lotus Chip:
    White Lotus is an organization that transcends the boundaries of the four nations, seeking philosophy, beauty, and truth. Zuko's Uncle Iroh is apart of the organization.
  3. Sword:
    The sword represents a time where Zuko struggles with his purpose. He creates this alter-ego by becoming a vigilante who wears a mask and carries two swords.
  4. Teacup/Teapot:
    Uncle Iroh loves tea! Uncle Iroh consistently provides Zuko with wisdom and guidance in Zuko's journey.

Final Screenshot

I want the audience to feel a sense of warmness from the candle light and peace with the minimal objects and empty space on the table. I want the audience to know that the character is in solitude and alone with his thoughts.

I chose a medium shot slightly above the table. I want the shot to be in the first person perspective of the character as if the character was sitting down at the table.

STAGE 2

Objective: Transfer objects into Unreal and add camera movements, sound, and animation

Story Board

Animation

The animation clip focuses on Zuko's internal conflict. I removed the sword and placed a Fire Nation royalty headpiece on the table to reflect his conflict. Objects representing evil/his past on the left and objects representing good on the right. I also included an audio clip which gives more context about Zuko.

STAGE 3

The final stage was a group project to create an interactive montage
